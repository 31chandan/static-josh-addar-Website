JOSH STATES "THE BALL IS ROLLING" ON THE NEW SINGLE

An email went out to everyone on the mailing list this morning and Josh stated the work has officially begun on the debut single "About A Girl". You can read that email for yourself below.

Hey ya’ll,

Josh here. Just wanted to touch base with everyone and bring you up to speed with the new single. Joe and I had a skype meeting with part of the team from Nashville yesterday to get things rolling on the song. I feel we walked away with a real feeling of the finished product. The song carries such a drive. We’re also going to add some rootsie elements as well. (Picture Keith Urban early 2000’s vibe… but current lol) We’re working with a ton of talented people on this project. To start with, the writers on this song alone, Josh Martin who was featured in Rolling Stone just this week for his new single “Cold Again”, Kylie Sackley who is nominated for songwriter of the year this year for the Canadian CMA’s, and singer/songwriter Andy Skib who is the lead singer of the rock band Midwest Kings and spent 2009 touring with American Idols David Cook. Not to mention all the musicians involved. There is a lot of talent on board and Joe and I are honestly pinching ourselves to have the opportunity to represent their work. Also, there a lot of belief from the team this song could be TOP 40 so we have a great group now beginning to reach out to their contacts to prepare it for Country Music Radio. So, it’s an understatement for me to say we are so excited to bring this to you.

This song was legitimately pitched to us by mistake. We were only listening to hear the quality of the song and I just lyrically fell in love with the song itself. I felt like it was a song that could really highlight who we are as artist. There’s so much pressure to release a song especially a debut single because your first single really sets the tone for who you are as artist. So, we have really tried to be extremely selective when it comes to this first song. However, the moment we heard the demo of About A Girl it was almost like I knew instantly that this was it. I felt it in my gut.

The guitar tracks and scratch vocals are being worked on as we speak. They’re supposed to send them to me for approval by this evening. Anyway, we love being able to communicate with you all so email us back! We love to hear from y’all.

Y’all just keep praying for us as things are coming together. Start getting excited and building hype about the release. Tell everyone you know! We can’t wait to see where this journey takes us.

J&J HOLLER PLAYS OLE RED GATLINBURG ALONG SIDE GRAND OLE OPRY MEMBER MARK WILLS

The stage was set and the stars were shining Friday evening for a power packed night full of fun times, good food and good music at Ole Red Gatlinburg.

The house was packed Friday night when J&J Holler took the stage at 8 PM to play their second live concert for Ole Red fans and county music seekers. They played an hour set before announcing country music recording artist and Grand Ole Opry member Mark Wills to take the stage.

The "Don't Laugh at Me" singer played an hour set and then J&J Holler took the stage again to wrap up the night.

The guys had an opportunity to hang out with Mark backstage and said it was an awesome experience.

"I grew up singing his songs. "19 Something" was my favorite songs to perform as a kid. So this night has been so nostalgic for me. Then to just sit down and have a conversation with him and he be so kind and down to earth really makes me believe in country music. Then to see him perform songs like "Jacobs Ladder" and "I Do"... I just felt like I was a 10 year old kid again. I still cant believe its real. This has truly been a night i'll remember for the rest of my life." -Josh

"Mark was such a down to earth guy! To have the opportunity to sit stage side and hear him sing one of his hit songs will always be a great memory. It was such a great evening." -Joe

J&J HOLLER PLAYED THE GRAND REOPENING OF BLAKE SHELTONS GATLINBURG TN RESTURANT OLE RED

      After the pause that the world was put on because of COVID-19, things are starting to come back alive in the country music state. On May 22nd the decision was made to open Blake Shelton's restaurant, Ole Red in Gatlinburg Tn. Ole Red is known for their amazing food, wonderful atmosphere and showcasing some of the finest country music artist in the country and on reopening night J&J Holler was there for the party! The duo was asked to bring in the celebration as they performed a three hour set for the re-opening. "It really was an awesome time, and they made us feel just like family" Josh said. "We look forward to establishing a relationship with this wonderful establishment and hopefully coming back in the future." He added.
